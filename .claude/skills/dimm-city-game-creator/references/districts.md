# Districts Quick Reference

## The Burrows
Cramped tunnels, makeshift shelters, communal survival. Rabbit warrens, rat kingdoms, outcasts.
**Challenges**: Collapses, flooding, gang territory, scarcity
**Opportunities**: Hidden routes, community, black markets

## Tech District
Corporate towers, glitching tech, Etherwave Transit Hub (cracked terminals, Drift Gate 7, Last Bite shop).
**Challenges**: Security sweeps, lockdowns, surveillance, system failures
**Opportunities**: Data access, transit, abandoned infrastructure

## Neon District
Gang wars, pleasure dens, chrome and blood. Cats vs Rabbits legendary conflict.
**Challenges**: Gang warfare, turf disputes, combat, betrayals
**Opportunities**: Black markets, fight winnings, gang alliances
**Faction**: Neon Lords crime syndicate

## The Wastes
Toxic ruins, bioluminescent fungi, mutated wildlife, Wastelander clans, Gutterdruid circles.
**Challenges**: Radiation, toxicity, structural instability, getting lost
**Opportunities**: Valuable salvage, rare materials, freedom

## The Dark
Reality unstable, spirits, glitches, etherlocks, existential horror.
**Challenges**: Reality glitches, mental corruption, possession, madness
**Opportunities**: Forbidden knowledge, impossible resources
